It’s Time For You To Build the Culture That Makes You Love To Go To Work

Welcome to Threads Culture. If you are a Manager, Owner or CEO who is looking for the real answers on culture, this is the right place.

We created this website because we have been in your shoes before and know it is next to impossible to sort through all the bullshit and people who want to get you on a $250 per hour treadmill to find the stuff that actually works. We went through years of listening to the “experts” recommended by our CEO/Owner friends and implementing the latest business books of the moment. It was mostly an exercise in wasted time.

We had to learn culture from getting our hands dirty and actually figuring out what worked. The information here is based on our real experiences leading teams, buying our own companies, starting our own companies and providing training to hundreds of organizations in every type of industry over the last 25 years.

Our goal with this site is to teach you everything you need to be a successful real-world expert with culture. All of our models, exercises, etc. are free to download without giving up your contact info. No calls to action, no landing pages, no pay wall, no hourly fees. Those things are about money… lead sources, sales pipelines and eventually selling you something. For our purposes here, that would cheapen what this is actually about.

So why did we create this site? We know what it feels like as a leader to have people on your team genuinely smile, thank you and tell you that they can’t imagine having to go work anywhere else. We want you to feel this as a leader. We want the people on your teams to experience what it is like to work in this kind of environment.

And if you are here now reading this, we know you have what it takes to make this happen in your team.

All of the posts below are organized by category if you have a specific area of focus, but also in the order we would proceed if you are starting from scratch.

Before you get started, we recommend that you read these two posts. While anyone can have a level of success with what we show you below, the deepest, most genuine rewards are available to those who take some time to get their hearts and minds right first.
